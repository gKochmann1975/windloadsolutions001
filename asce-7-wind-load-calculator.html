ASCE 7 Editions Comparison

ASCE 7-16

Current Standard (2021 IBC)

  • Updated wind speed maps with refined regional data
  • Enhanced topographic provisions
  • Rooftop solar panel provisions in Chapter 29
  • Clarified C&C calculation procedures
  • Widely adopted nationwide

ASCE 7-22

Latest Edition (2024 IBC)

  • Completely revised wind speed maps (700-year return period)
  • Updated hurricane wind speeds
  • Modified topographic factor methodology
  • Revised internal pressure coefficients
  • Simplified low-rise building procedures
