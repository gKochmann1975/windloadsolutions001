Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Building Intelligence Platform

How does the 3 searches/week free tier work?

You get 3 uses per week across ALL 5 tools—not 3 per tool. Each search (whether Velocity Finder, Hurricane Risk, or any other tool) counts as 1 use. Your count resets every 7 days from your first use. No credit card required to start.

What counts as a "search"?

One search = one lookup/calculation. Examples: Velocity Finder (1 ZIP code = 1 search), Hurricane Risk (1 location = 1 search), Multi-ZIP Compare (up to 10 ZIPs = 1 search), Solar Site Finder (1 filtered search = 1 search).

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime?

Yes! Upgrade instantly for immediate access. Downgrade at any time—changes take effect at your next billing cycle. No penalties, no commitments.

Is the data ASCE 7-22 compliant?

Absolutely. Our Velocity Finder uses official ASCE 7-22 wind speed maps, Florida Building Code 2023, and verified local authority amendments. All calculations are permit-ready.

Where does the hurricane data come from?

We aggregate 75 years (1950-2024) of data from NOAA, National Hurricane Center, and historical archives—then our AI analyzes patterns for risk scoring.

Can I export my data?

Yes! All plans include PDF reports. Professional and Enterprise plans include CSV export for Advanced Search and Multi-ZIP Compare.

Do you offer API access for developers?

Enterprise plans include 1,000 API calls/month. Need more? Custom plans offer unlimited API access with SLA guarantees.

What if I need help?

Free tier: Email support (24-48 hr response). Professional: Priority email (12 hr response). Enterprise: Phone + chat support (4 hr response). Custom: Dedicated account manager.
