Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after my free trial?

Your trial expires after 7 days. To continue using the platform, simply choose a paid plan. Your trial data and settings are saved.

Can I change plans later?

Yes! Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect immediately.

Do I need a credit card for the free trial?

No. Start your free trial with just your email. Add payment only when you're ready to subscribe.

What's the difference between Pro and Premium?

Pro includes 250 calculations/month with 5 team members. Premium offers unlimited calculations, 15 team members, AI Hurricane Intelligence, natural language search, priority support, and API access.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees. Pay only your monthly or annual subscription.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) through our secure Stripe payment processor.
