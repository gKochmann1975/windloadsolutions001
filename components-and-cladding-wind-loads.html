Understanding C&C Pressure Zones

Wall Zones

Zone 4 - Interior Areas

  • Located away from building corners
  • Lower pressures due to uniform flow
  • Covers most wall surface area

Zone 5 - Edge & Corner Zones

  • Within 10% of building dimension from corners
  • 2x higher pressures than interior zones
  • Critical for window and cladding design

Roof Zones

Zone 1 - Interior Roof

  • Central roof area away from edges
  • Lowest roof pressures

Zone 2 - Edge Strips

  • Perimeter strips along roof edges
  • Elevated suction pressures

Zone 3 - Corner Zones

  • Square areas at roof corners
  • Highest pressures - often govern design
  • 3-4x higher than interior zones
